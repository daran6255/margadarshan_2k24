About The Event

Join us on a transformative journey at our job Mela, 'Unlocking Potential: Paving the Path to Inclusive Employment.' We celebrate diversity and firmly believe in the untapped potential of every individual, regardless of their abilities.

Our goal is to spotlight the unique skills of Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) and actively break down workforce barriers. Together, we promise a brighter, more inclusive future where everyone can thrive and contribute to society, irrespective of their abilities.

    FAQ

    • Candidates
    • Companies
    • What is the MargaDarshan Job Mela?

      The MargaDarshan Job Mela, organized by the WinVinaya Foundation, is a unique recruitment event connecting you with companies actively seeking talented individuals with disabilities!

    • Am I eligible to attend?

      Absolutely! MargaDarshan Job Mela are open to persons with disabilities (PwDs) who have been trained by WinVinaya, as well as any other individuals with disabilities seeking employment.

    • How can I participate?

      Click the "Candidate Registration" button on the website to register for the job mela.

    • How should I prepare for the mela?

      1. Research companies attending the mela (background, values, job openings).
      2. Practice your interview skills (attend training offered by WinVinaya Foundation).
      3. Dress professionally to make a great first impression.
      4. Prepare talking points highlighting your strengths, relevant experience, and career goals.
      5. Bring an organized portfolio with copies of your resume, education certificates, experience certificates, and the disability certificate.
      6. Be professional and polite throughout the event.

    • What can I expect at the mela?

      1. Meet with companies committed to hiring PwDs.
      2. Receive free guidance and information on job opportunities aligned with your skills.
      3. Interview preparation sessions to boost your confidence.
      4. Get a chance to interview with multiple companies, at least two for each candidate.
      5. Highlight your abilities to a wide range of potential employers.
      6. Network with other PwDs, organizations, and helpful volunteers.

    • What should I bring to a MargaDarshan job mela?

      1. Five copies of your updated, tailored resume 2. One government ID 3. Disability certificate 4. Education certificates 5. Work experience certificates You can bring the Xerox copies of the above-mentioned documents.

    • What types of jobs are available at MargaDarshan job mela?

      We connect with a diverse range of inclusive companies, including multinational corporations (MNCs), micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs), startups and NGOs. Available job roles encompass both IT and non-IT positions, providing a variety of opportunities for all participants.

    • What is the MargaDarshan Job Mela?

      The MargaDarshan Job Mela, organized by the WinVinaya Foundation, is an exceptional opportunity to recruit pre-screened, highly skilled candidates with disabilities.

    • Can my company participate?

      Yes, if your company is committed to diversity and inclusion by hiring persons with disabilities (PwDs), you are welcome to recruit at the MargaDarshan Job Mela.

    • How can we prepare for the mela?

      Click the "Corporate Registration" button on the website to register for the event. Our placement team will contact you to discuss the specific hiring needs.

    • How can my company get involved?

      Here is how to ensure a successful and inclusive recruitment experience:
      1. Connect with WinVinaya to discuss your hiring needs and explore the available talent pool.
      2. Participate in WinVinaya's disability inclusion workshops to enhance your team's understanding.
      3. Collaborate with WinVinaya on job mapping to match candidates' skills with your open positions.
      4. Train your interviewers on best practices for conducting disability-inclusive interviews
      5. Review and refine job descriptions to focus on essential skills and qualifications, avoiding language that might unintentionally exclude PwD candidates.

    • What can we expect at the mela?

      1. Access Top Talent: Meet and recruit skilled PWD candidates ready for roles in Software Development, Software Testing, MIS, Financial Accounting, and more.
      2. Fireside Chat: Network with industry leaders and learn best practices for inclusive hiring.
      3. Experience Zone: Connect with people with disabilities and explore through fun activities.

      • Sensory Safari: Step into a world without sight to gain a new perspective.
      • ISL Sprint: Learn 15 basic phrases in Indian Sign Language (ISL) in just 15minutes.
      • Soul Speak: Enjoy artwork that highlights the immense talent of people with hearing and speech impairments.
      4. Free Training: Recruiters get exclusive access to WinVinaya's Online Disability Awareness course.

    • What should I bring to a MargaDarshan job mela?

      Come prepared with positivity, enthusiasm, an open-minded, and learning-oriented approach. Have a team of knowledgeable and friendly representatives available to answer questions, conduct interviews, and discuss your company's disability inclusion initiatives.
